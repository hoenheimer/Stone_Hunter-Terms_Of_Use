Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2025

Thank you for using Stone Hunter, a mobile application developed to help users quit smoking and track their nicotine consumption progress.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Stone Hunter. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the EU, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Data You Provide

We do not require users to create an account to use Stone Hunter. However, you may choose to voluntarily provide personal data, such as:

  • Smoking/vaping intake records
  • Quit plans or goals
  • Health-related inputs (e.g., cravings, moods, triggers)
  • Notes, tags, or personal reflections

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain non-identifiable data:

  • App usage statistics (e.g., frequency of use, session length)
  • Device type, OS version, and crash logs (for performance and bug fixing)

1.3 Apple Health Data (Optional)

If you opt-in, Stone Hunter may integrate with Apple Health to read or write relevant data (e.g., mindfulness minutes, respiratory rate). This is only used locally and not shared externally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide personalized support for your quit journey
  • Track and visualize your progress
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Respond to user inquiries and feedback

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Optional iCloud backup is available if you are logged into your Apple ID and have enabled it.
  • We implement industry-standard security practices to protect data from unauthorized access or loss.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Under GDPR (EU residents):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Right to object or restrict processing

Under CCPA/CPRA (California residents):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell your data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: stonehunter-feedback@omis.app

5. Third-Party Services

Stone Hunter may use limited third-party services (e.g., crash reporting or analytics tools such as Apple’s built-in diagnostics). These providers are contractually required to comply with privacy regulations and use data only as necessary.

6. Children’s Privacy

Stone Hunter is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in-app or on our website with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: stonehunter-feedback@omis.app
